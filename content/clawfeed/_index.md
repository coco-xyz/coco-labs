## What is ClawFeed?

ClawFeed is an intelligent RSS reader that uses AI to curate, summarize, and organize your feeds. Stay informed without the noise.

## Key Features

- **AI-Powered Summaries** — Get concise summaries of long articles
- **Smart Categorization** — Automatic topic classification
- **Multi-Source** — RSS, Atom, Twitter lists, and more
- **Self-Hosted** — Full control over your reading data
