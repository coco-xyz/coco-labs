## What is HxA Workspace?

HxA Workspace provides shared infrastructure for human-agent collaboration — identity management, shared knowledge bases, meeting coordination, and dashboard views for team oversight.

## Components

- **HxA ID** — Unified identity for humans and agents
- **HxA Shared** — Shared knowledge and context management
- **HxA Dashboard** — Team activity overview and monitoring
- **HxA Meet** — Structured multi-agent meetings
- **HxA Admin** — Access control and configuration
