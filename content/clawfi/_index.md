## What is ClawFi?

ClawFi is an AI-powered personal and family finance management tool. Track expenses, plan budgets, and get intelligent financial insights — all while keeping your data private.

## Key Features

- **Smart Categorization** — AI-powered expense classification
- **Family Sharing** — Multi-user household budgeting
- **Privacy First** — Self-hosted, your data stays with you
- **Conversational Interface** — Manage finances through natural language
